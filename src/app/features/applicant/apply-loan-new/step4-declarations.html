Purpose: To verify your identity and comply with regulatory requirements under the Prevention of Money Laundering Act (PMLA) and RBI guidelines.

What we will do:

Data Security: All KYC data is encrypted and stored in compliance with data protection laws. Your information will not be shared with third parties except as required by law.

2. Credit Bureau Report & Credit Score Assessment

Purpose: To assess your creditworthiness, repayment capacity, and credit history for determining loan eligibility and interest rates.

What we will access:

  • Credit Bureaus: CIBIL (TransUnion), Experian, Equifax, and CRIF High Mark
  • Credit Score: Your credit score ranging from 300-900 (higher is better)
  • Credit History: Past and current loans, credit cards, payment history, defaults, and settlements
  • Credit Inquiries: Recent credit applications and hard inquiries made by other lenders
  • Public Records: Bankruptcies, foreclosures, civil suits, and judgments (if any)

Impact on Credit Score: This will be recorded as a "hard inquiry" and may temporarily impact your credit score by 5-10 points. Multiple loan applications within a short period may further reduce your score.

Your Rights: You have the right to obtain a free credit report annually from each bureau. You can dispute any inaccuracies in your credit report.

Retention: Credit inquiry records are retained by credit bureaus for 2 years as per RBI guidelines.

3. Bank Statement Retrieval & Financial Analysis

Purpose: To verify your income, assess your financial stability, spending patterns, and repayment capacity.

What we will analyze:

  • Income Verification: Salary credits, business income, rental income, and other regular deposits
  • Banking Behavior: Average monthly balance, account maintenance, cheque bounces, and overdraft usage
  • Expense Analysis: EMI payments, credit card payments, utility bills, and discretionary spending
  • Cash Flow Pattern: Inflow vs outflow ratio to determine surplus income available for loan repayment
  • Red Flags: Frequent loan applications, gambling transactions, cryptocurrency trading, or suspicious activities

Statement Period: We typically require 3-6 months of bank statements. For business loans, 12 months may be required.

Multiple Accounts: If you have multiple bank accounts, we may request statements from all accounts used for salary/income credits.

Data Privacy: Bank statements are analyzed using automated systems with bank-grade encryption. Access is limited to authorized personnel only.

Account Aggregator Framework: We use RBI-approved Account Aggregator services for secure, consent-based access to your financial data.
